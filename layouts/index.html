Mission and Value

Who we are and how this approach works for small, resource-constrained organizations.

What we do

We provide hosting, maintenance, and one content update per month for a limited number of nonprofit websites.

This is not a traditional web agency model. It is a small, donation-supported program.

Who benefits

  • Small nonprofits that need a simple informational website
  • Organizations comfortable with limited monthly edits
  • Teams that want long‑term stability without technical overhead
  • Groups able to manage their own domain registration

How It Works

A simple overview of how the program works.

  1. What we provide

    Hosting, secure setup, routine maintenance, and one content update per month are covered by donations.

  2. What your nonprofit covers

    Your organization maintains ownership of its domain name. Typical annual cost: $12–$20 per year.

  3. Capacity policy

    We operate within a modest monthly infrastructure budget. When hosting capacity is reached, new organizations join a waitlist.

How this work is supported

OhanaBuilds is sustained through donations that cover hosting and maintenance for a limited number of partner organizations.

The only direct cost most nonprofits pay is their domain name. There are no hidden fees. If capacity is reached, intake pauses until resources allow expansion.
