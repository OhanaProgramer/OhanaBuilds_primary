Mission and Value

Who we are and how this approach works for small, resource-constrained organizations.

OhanaBuilds was created to apply professional web experience to organizations that serve their communities but lack technical resources.

What we do

OhanaBuilds builds and launches your website at no cost.

We handle the technical setup, hosting structure, and long‑term stability so your team does not have to manage infrastructure or software.

If we cannot fully fund and support a project responsibly, we do not take it on.

Who benefits

  • Small nonprofits that need a simple informational website
  • Organizations comfortable with limited monthly edits
  • Teams that want long‑term stability without technical overhead
  • Community groups that need a clear, professional web presence
