George AI

An OhanaBuilds project for fitness, recovery, and wearable-data analytics

George AI is an early-stage analytics platform focused on training readiness, recovery, and long-term health trends using wearable data.

Mission

The founder is a disabled military veteran building this initially for personal rehabilitation, recovery, and long-term performance tracking, with a broader goal to help other veterans and recovery-focused users with clearer data and practical insights.

Planned Features

  • Wearable data trend dashboards
  • Training and recovery summaries
  • Readiness and long-term health tracking
  • Coaching-style insights

Privacy and Consent

Wearable data is only used with explicit user consent and is not sold.
