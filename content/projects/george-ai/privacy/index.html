George AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to George AI, an OhanaBuilds project.

George AI is an early-stage project focused on fitness, recovery, and wearable-data analytics. This policy explains, in plain language, what information may be collected, how it may be used, and the choices users have.

1. Who we are

George AI is an OhanaBuilds project. It is being developed as an analytics platform for training readiness, recovery, and long-term health trend tracking using wearable and fitness data.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how George AI is used, we may collect:

  • Contact information you provide (such as name and email address).
  • Wearable or fitness data if you choose to connect supported platforms (for example, Garmin, Strava, or similar services).
  • Technical logs and usage data (such as device/browser details, timestamps, and basic diagnostic information) to operate and troubleshoot the service.

3. How we use information

We may use information to:

  • Provide dashboards and analytics views.
  • Generate training, recovery, and readiness summaries.
  • Provide coaching-style insights and trend reporting.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the service.
  • Respond to support requests and user communications.

4. Consent and connected platforms

George AI requires explicit user authorization before accessing data from Garmin, Strava, or other connected wearable platforms. You control whether to connect a platform, and you may revoke access through the platform settings and/or by contacting us.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information only when necessary to operate the service (for example, with hosting or infrastructure providers), to comply with legal obligations, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the service.

6. Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably needed for service operation, analytics features, support, and legal or operational requirements. We will honor deletion requests where feasible and consistent with legal obligations.

8. User rights / deletion requests

You may request access updates or deletion of your information by contacting Email us. We will review requests and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Third-party services

George AI may rely on third-party services such as Garmin, Strava, and hosting or analytics providers (if used). Those services operate under their own terms and privacy policies, and users should review them separately.
